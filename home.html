Helping founders to get ahead

About programme

Tech Twin Towns: Berlin - Tel Aviv (TTT) is a private initiative that aims to give founders from Tel Aviv and Berlin a headstart as they move their startups from the respective cities. The initiative offers founders three benefits:

  • Facilitated introductions to key members from the respective startup communities

    It’s not easy if you are new founder in town to get meetings with angel investors, decision makers of potential clients, etc. To lessen this challenge that we’ve got the commitment from some of the leading ecosystem players to meet TTT founders. All you’ve got to do is to apply via eMail by answering some simple questions.

  • Raising the awareness of what startup events are going on in each city

    Either if you are considering to move to either town or looking for ways how to expand your startup’s business, it’s not simple to gather an awareness of the events going on. To address that, in cooperation with Geektime (Tel Aviv) and Gründerszene (Berlin) we’re a compiling a newsletter that highlights the key startup events in each month. You can subscribe to the newsletter and make us aware of a relevant event via eMail.

  • Regular events.

    We will be hosting regular events in both cities. Details coming up.
